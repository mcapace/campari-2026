The Big Idea

Every great spirit has a season. Every season has a story. And every story deserves a stage worthy of the brand behind it.

The Campari Collection is a premium branded digital destination living natively within Wine Spectator and Whisky Advocate — two of the most trusted consumer spirits media brands in the world. It's not a banner. It's not an ad unit. It's a destination where Campari Group's portfolio comes alive, brand by brand, moment by moment, speaking directly to the most engaged spirits consumers in the country.

M. Shanken builds and hosts a fully custom branded hub at winespectator.com/campari and whiskyadvocate.com/campari — designed to feel like a natural, premium extension of both editorial environments. The creative rotates with the calendar. The audience stays captive. The brands stay top of mind. All hub design, development, content production, hosting, and campaign management is handled entirely by M. Shanken. Campari Group supplies brand assets, creative guidelines, and any video content they wish to incorporate.

The Experience

Built Like a Magazine. Engineered Like a Platform.

When a consumer lands on the hub, they arrive in a world that is unmistakably Campari Group — filtered through the authority and aesthetic of the Shanken brand. It feels curated, not commercial. The architecture works in two layers that work together seamlessly.

Layer 1 — The Seasonal Hero

The front door of the hub changes with the calendar. Whatever activation is live takes center stage — a full-width immersive experience built around that brand's moment. Bold art direction, the season's key cocktail serve front and center, and a clear consumer action: make this drink, explore this brand, find a bottle. Where the client has supplied video assets, they run in the hero — full-bleed, autoplay, silent — creating a cinematic first impression that static creative simply cannot match.

Layer 2 — The Portfolio Navigation

Below the hero, a persistent brand navigation bar lets consumers move between every Campari Group brand at any time. Each brand has its own chapter — a dedicated section with its own visual identity, color palette, and story. Espolon's chapter feels nothing like Courvoisier's, which feels nothing like Aperol's. But they all live under the same roof, with the same premium production quality and the same seamless experience.

The Activations
Espolon + Grand Marnier
The Grand Margarita. Perfected.
Cinco de Mayo  ·  April – May
"Two icons. One legendary cocktail."
The Grand Margarita — Espolon Blanco, Grand Marnier, fresh lime — positioned not as a variation but as the definitive serve. The hub chapter explodes with color and culture: Mexican heritage, craft tequila storytelling, and the unmistakable French elegance Grand Marnier brings to the rim. Recipe content, cocktail inspiration, and cultural celebration come together in Campari Group's biggest spring moment. Where the client has lifestyle video of the pour, it runs in the hero.
Drivers

E-Blast to Wine Spectator's consumer list two weeks before May 5th

Paid Social — Instagram and Facebook running cocktail creative and recipe content

Programmatic targeting tequila lovers, cocktail culture audiences, and Cinco entertaining intent

Display on WineSpectator.com on launch day

Wild Turkey
The Pour That Says Everything.
Father's Day  ·  June
"Some gifts say a lot. The right bourbon says everything."
Wild Turkey's Father's Day chapter is warm, cinematic, and deeply personal — rooted in Kentucky heritage and the timeless ritual of sharing a great pour with someone who deserves it. A curated gift guide format makes it easy to act on. Aspirational lifestyle imagery makes it impossible to scroll past.
Drivers

E-Blast to Whisky Advocate subscribers — the ideal bourbon-literate audience

Paid Social — Facebook and Instagram targeting men 35–60 and active gift shoppers

Programmatic gift-intent and bourbon-lover targeting

Newsletter sponsorship across WA throughout June

The hub chapter is bold, bright, and built for sharing. Aperol's iconic orange is everywhere. The message is simple: this is what summer looks and tastes like. Client-supplied outdoor lifestyle video integrates seamlessly into the hero where available.

The Driver Stack

Four Channels. One Cohesive Program.

Dedicated E-Blasts

The highest-impact driver in the program. Each activation gets a dedicated send to Wine Spectator and/or Whisky Advocate's subscriber lists — the most qualified spirits consumers in the country. Subject lines and creative are activation-specific, brand-authentic, and built to drive traffic directly to the live hub chapter.

Paid Social — Instagram & Facebook

Seasonal creative runs across Wine Spectator and Whisky Advocate social channels and extends via paid amplification to lookalike and interest-based audiences. Each activation has its own visual direction. Client-supplied video assets are incorporated where available. Aperol and Espolon carry the heaviest social investment given their visual and cultural resonance.

Programmatic Display

Precision-targeted campaigns across the open web extend each activation's reach beyond the Shanken ecosystem. Retargeting pixels on the hub recapture visitors who have already shown intent. Audience segments shift by activation window — tequila lovers, bourbon collectors, lifestyle consumers, gift shoppers, luxury buyers. Always working. Always optimizing.

High-Impact Display

Premium display units on WineSpectator.com and WhiskyAdvocate.com — roadblocks, high-impact placements, and newsletter sponsorships — timed to each activation launch to drive the initial traffic surge to the hub and maintain always-on brand visibility between peaks.
