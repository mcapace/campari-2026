1. Audience profile across M. Shanken digital and print properties

Please provide background on the audience profile across the M. Shanken digital and print properties (e.g., trade vs. consumer, interests).

Consumer-focused properties (Wine Spectator, Whisky Advocate, Cigar Aficionado) reach affluent, educated consumers who are highly engaged with wine, spirits, and lifestyle content. Demographics are summarized below.

Trade-focused properties (Impact, Market Watch, Shanken News Daily, Trade E-Blast) reach the three-tier trade: wholesalers, retailers, and on-premise operators. Impact and Market Watch are read by distributor and supplier executives, chain buyers, and independent retail/on-premise decision-makers for programming, distribution, and placement.

7. Impact Seminar

Please share more information on the Impact Seminar.

The Impact Seminar is a global marketing conference attended by 250–275 executives in the beverage alcohol industry, including:

  • Wholesaler principals and executives
  • Supplier principals and sales and marketing executives
  • Retail owners
  • Financial industry executives

There are typically six presentations on various topics, presented to the audience by selected industry executives (Campari has presented several times over the years). Following the seminars there is a lunch with a prominent speaker—usually a political or business leader, or occasionally a media personality. Past speakers have included President George W. Bush, Bill Clinton, Michael Milken, General David Petraeus, Bret Baier, and others.
